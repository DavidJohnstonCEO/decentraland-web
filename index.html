About

Decentraland aims to build a decentralized virtual reality; where proof-of-work allows you to claim land; you own the physical rules of your estate.

Alpha Prototype: Stone Age

When you mine a block, control over the color of one pixel is yours, in a shared visualization of the blockchain. Transactions are used to change the colors of the pixels.
