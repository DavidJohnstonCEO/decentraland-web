Decentraland
A blockchain based virtual reality world.

About

Decentraland aims to build a decentralized virtual reality; where new land gets mined on each block and its physical rules are determined by its owner.

Experiment 1

Decentrapixel: When users mine a block, they gain control over the color of one pixel in a shared visualization of the blockchain. Transactions can be used to change the colors of the pixels.
